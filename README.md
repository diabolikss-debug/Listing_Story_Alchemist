## Writing Philosophy

The skill follows these principles:

- **Buyers buy feelings, not features.** Every listing starts with an emotional scene, not a product pitch.
- **SEO and emotion are partners, not opposites.** Keywords are woven into natural copy.
- **Specific beats vague.** "Hand-knotted with 4mm Egyptian cotton rope" always wins over "made with premium materials."
- **No AI-sounding language.** The output avoids words like "delve," "elevate," "curated," "bespoke," and other patterns that signal machine-generated text.
- **No em dashes.** Consistent with the author's style preferences.

## 5. SEASONAL/TREND ADAPTATION

- **Current angle (April/Spring 2026):** AI art tool updates typically drop in Q2. Position as "works with the latest models" and mention compatibility updates. Buyers searching for prompts spike after major model releases.
- **Trending keyword:** "consistent character AI prompts" is gaining search volume as creators want to build recurring characters for comics, stories, and social media. Consider adding a "character consistency" angle to the description.
- **Evergreen product note:** AI prompt packs don't have strong seasonal cycles. Performance is driven by AI tool hype cycles and model releases. Monitor Midjourney and Stable Diffusion release dates and run promotions within the first week of any major update.
- **Relaunch trigger:** When Midjourney v7 or the next Stable Diffusion version drops, update the pack, add "v7 compatible" to the title, and re-promote. This alone can double sales for 2-3 weeks.

## 6. COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

**Top search terms:**
- "anime ai prompts midjourney"
- "ai art prompt pack female character"
- "stable diffusion anime prompt bundle"

**What top results look like:** Most competitor packs offer 50-200 prompts at $3-7, rely on quantity over quality, show generic preview grids, and have minimal descriptions. Titles are keyword-stuffed. Few explain their testing process or offer updates.

**Differentiation angles:**
1. **Quality over quantity.** 20 tested prompts beats 200 untested ones. Make the testing process visible: "50+ generations per prompt" is a concrete proof point that most competitors can't claim.
2. **Free updates.** Most prompt packs are fire-and-forget. Offering updates when new AI models release creates ongoing value and justifies a higher price point.

**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Keyword-stuffed titles with no readability ("anime girl prompt midjourney prompt stable diffusion prompt ai art prompt pack bundle")
- No preview images showing actual outputs from the prompts
- Listing 100+ prompts when 80% produce nearly identical results
- No negative prompt guidance (buyers don't know what to exclude)
