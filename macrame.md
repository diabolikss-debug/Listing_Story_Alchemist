**What you get:**
- One macrame wall hanging, approximately 24" wide x 30" long
- Mounted on a natural birch dowel
- Made with 4mm single-strand cotton rope, unbleached
- Ready to hang (dowel has pre-attached cord)

Each piece varies slightly because that's how handwork works. The one you receive will have its own character, which is the entire point.

Your walls have been waiting for something that feels intentional. This is that something.

## 6. COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

- **Top search terms:** "macrame wall hanging large," "boho wall decor handmade," "woven wall art"
- **What top results look like:** Clean product photos on white/neutral backgrounds. Multiple angle shots. Styled room mockups. Listings heavy on specifications but light on story.
- **Differentiation:** Most competitors list specs and materials. Almost nobody tells a story. The handwork hours (6-8) is a powerful differentiator. Lead with process, not just product.
- **Common mistakes:** Generic titles like "Beautiful Wall Hanging," no lifestyle photos, missing dimensions, tags that repeat title words.

## 7. PRICING INSIGHT

- **Low tier:** $35-45 (competing on price, commodity positioning)
- **Sweet spot:** $55-75 (handmade premium, story-justified)
- **Premium:** $85-120 (with the 2X listing, gift packaging, custom sizing)
- **Logic:** 6-8 hours of labor at any reasonable hourly rate puts material + time cost above $30. The $55-75 range respects the craft without pricing out impulse buyers.
- **Bundling:** Consider a "boho starter kit" bundle with 2-3 smaller pieces at a slight discount. Bundles increase average order value by 20-40% on Etsy.
