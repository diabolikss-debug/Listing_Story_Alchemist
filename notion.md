## 6. COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

- **Top search terms:** "freelance notion template," "notion invoice tracker," "freelance business dashboard notion"
- **What top results look like:** Clean screenshots of the template, feature lists, often priced between $15-29. Many lack video walkthroughs or real-world examples.
- **Differentiation:** Most competitors show features. Few show the transformation ("before/after" of freelance chaos vs. organized business). The "built by a freelancer" angle is common but the specific pain points (11pm invoice hunting) are rare.
- **Common mistakes:** No demo/preview, vague feature descriptions ("track everything!"), no setup time estimate, missing screenshots of actual template views.

## 7. PRICING INSIGHT

- **Low tier:** $9-15 (volume play, low perceived value)
- **Sweet spot:** $19-29 (serious tool positioning, covers the value of hours saved)
- **Premium:** $39-49 (with video walkthrough, quarterly update promise, community access)
- **Logic:** A freelancer billing $50+/hour saves 2-3 hours per month with this template. At $29, payback period is under one billing cycle.
- **Bundling:** Pair with a "Client Onboarding Template" or "Freelance Proposal Template" for a $39 bundle. Increases AOV and creates ecosystem lock-in.
